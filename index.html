How to Play TyperRacer
  1. Select a difficulty level from the dropdown menu (Easy, Medium, or Hard).
  2. Start typing the text shown in the prompt area. The timer will begin automatically when you type your first character.
  3. Watch your progress — correctly typed words turn blue, incorrect words turn red.
  4. Press Enter when you're finished to stop the test and see your results.
  5. Click Retry to try again with a new random text.

Tip: Focus on accuracy first — only correctly typed words count towards your WPM score!
